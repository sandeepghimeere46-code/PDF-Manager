Accessibility Statement
Our commitment to accessible, inclusive design.
Effective Date: 1 January 2025  •  Open Vision Engineering

Our Commitment

Open Vision Engineering is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities, including those who are blind or visually impaired. We continually improve the user experience for everyone by applying and advancing relevant accessibility standards.

Accessibility is not an afterthought for Open Vision — it is a core design principle. PDF Manager is built to work effectively with screen readers, keyboard navigation, and other assistive technologies.

Measures We Take

  • No emoji or icon-only labels are used in the interface — all interface elements use descriptive text
  • All interactive elements are reachable and operable by keyboard alone
  • Form inputs, buttons, and links include accessible labels and ARIA attributes where needed
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios are maintained throughout the interface
  • Status messages and results are announced to screen readers using ARIA live regions
  • The application avoids relying on color alone to convey information
  • Font sizes are specified in relative units to respect user browser settings
  • Success audio feedback is provided for completed actions

Conformance Status

PDF Manager aims to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. We are actively working to identify and address any areas that fall short of this standard.

Screen Reader Compatibility

PDF Manager is designed and tested for compatibility with the following screen readers and browsers:

  • NVDA with Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome on Windows
  • JAWS with Google Chrome on Windows
  • VoiceOver with Safari on macOS and iOS
  • TalkBack with Chrome on Android
  • Jieshuo screen reader on Android

Known Limitations

Some third-party PDF rendering libraries used by PDF Manager (such as PDF.js and Tesseract.js) may produce output that is not fully optimised for screen reader consumption. We provide text-based alternatives and download options wherever possible.

Feedback and Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of PDF Manager. If you experience any barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact Open Vision Engineering through our official channels. We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days.

Formal Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response to an accessibility concern, you may contact relevant national accessibility enforcement bodies in your region.

Our Mission

OpenVision Engineering Accessibility. Empowering Innovation. We believe that powerful tools should be available and usable by everyone, regardless of ability.
